# BDR Persona: The AI Solution Architect
**Version:** 1.0 | 

## 1. Who is Alex Morgan?
Alex is not a traditional "smile and dial" rep. Alex is a **hybrid**—part sales hunter, part tech enthusiast. Alex sells to CIOs, CTOs, and Heads of Data, but does not speak purely in jargon. 

Alex's superpower is **translating complex model accuracy into business revenue**. Alex knows that a 2% increase in prediction accuracy means $2M in saved inventory costs for a manufacturing client.

## 2. The "AI" Difference (Why this persona is unique)
Selling AI is different from selling standard SaaS. 

- **The Trust Gap:** Buyers are scared of "hallucinations" and bias. 
- **The Data Moat:** Alex doesn't just ask "What is your budget?"; Alex asks "How is your unstructured data being stored right now?" 
- **The Pilot:** Alex focuses on winning a 3-month proof-of-concept (POC), not the $500k deal upfront.

## 3. How to Engage this Persona (If you are Marketing)
If you are creating content for Alex Morgan, remember they are time-poor and metric-obsessed. 

- **Do:** Send them a 1-pager comparing your AI model's latency/accuracy vs. competitors.
- **Do:** Provide pre-approved email templates that address the EU AI Act or HIPAA compliance.
- **Don't:** Send them gated whitepapers that are 50 pages long. They will not read them.
- **Don't:** Send generic "Happy Friday" emails. They delete them.

## 4. Qualifying Criteria (BANT + AI)
Alex uses a modified BANT framework. When speaking to a lead, Alex must check these boxes before passing to an AE:

- **B**udget: Are they allocating a specific line item for "AI Transformation" in Q4?
- **A**uthority: Are we speaking to the Economic Buyer (CIO) or just a Director of IT?
- **N**eed: Do they have a specific **unstructured data** problem (e.g., PDFs, images, logs) that a rules-based system can't handle?
- **T**imeline: Do they want to start a POC within 30 days? (If no, nurture sequence).

## 5. Tools of the Trade (Stack)
To mirror Alex's workflow, ensure your tech stack is integrated:

1.  **Prospecting:** ZoomInfo + LinkedIn Sales Navigator.
2.  **Engagement:** Outreach.io for sequences; Loom for personalized video intros.
3.  **Research:** Alex uses ChatGPT to summarize an annual report in 30 seconds to find the company's strategic goals before a call.
4.  **CRM:** Salesforce (activity logged within 2 hours of the call, no exceptions).

## 6. Call Script Cheat Sheet (The First 60 Seconds)
> *"Hi [Name], this is Alex from [Company]. I know you're busy. I'm not calling to sell you a license. I noticed you recently posted about [Insert LinkedIn Trigger]... 
> We helped [Competitor Company] solve a similar issue where their legacy system failed to classify 15% of support tickets. I have a 2-minute demo showing how we used a small language model to fix that. Can I share my screen?"

## 7. Success Metrics (KPIs)
Alex's performance is measured not just by demos booked, but by **Quality**.

- **Metric 1:** Qualified Opportunities Created (SQLs) - *Target: 8-10/month.*
- **Metric 2:** Conversion Rate from Discovery Call to POC - *Target: >40%.*
- **Metric 3:** Average Deal Size of Sourced Pipeline - *Target: >$100k ACV.*

---

**Note to Managers:** Because the AI landscape changes weekly, Alex requires **1-hour weekly** training on new model capabilities. Without this, Alex loses credibility with CTOs. Feed Alex, and Alex will feed the pipeline.
